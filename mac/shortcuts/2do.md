# 2Do

* [https://www.2doapp.com/](https://www.2doapp.com/)

## Sync

* `⌘ s`

## View

* `⌘ 0` Inbox
* _`⌘ 1` All_
* `⌘ 2` Today
* _`⌘ 3` Starred_
* _`⌘ 4` Scheduled_
* _`⌘ 5` Done_
* _`⌘ ↑` Prev List_
* _`⌘ ↓` Next List_
* _`⌘ ⌥ ↑|↓` \[ Collapse \| Expand \] All Projects_
* _`⌘ ⇧ ↑|↓` \[ Collapse \| Expand \] All List Groups_

## Edit

* `0 ~ 3` Priority : None \| Low \| Medium \| High
* `s` Star
* `^ s` Unstar
* `d` Due Date
* `e` Due Time
* `k` Schedule \( Start Date \)
* `t` Start Today
* `^ t` Due Today
* `y` Start Yesterday
* `⌘ /` Dates
* `⌘ e` Notes
* `⌘ ^ ⇧ 1` Convert to a Task
* `⌘ ^ ⇧ 2` Convert to a Project
* `⌘ ^ ⇧ 3` Convert to a Checklist
* `⌘ l` Move to another list
* `⌘ .` Mark as Completed
* `⌥ ⌘ .` Mark as Not Completed
* `⌘ ;` Recurrence \( aka Repeat \)
* `⌘ '` Alerts
* _`l` Tag_
* _`⌘ r` Tags_
* _`^ ⌘ ↑|↓` Move Up \| Down_
